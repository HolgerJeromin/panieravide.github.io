OpenStreetMap opening hours made easy !
Just drag time intervals on the calendar, and we will do the job.

opening_hours=

To add an interval, drag the cursor on the wanted interval on the calendar.
You can move an interval with drag and drop.
You can resize one by extending its borders.
You can delete one by clicking on it.
